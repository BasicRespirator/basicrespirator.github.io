Safety

This respirator is not certified and is only offered as a potential solution to meet immediate needs during a crisis.

No open source respirator has been NIOSH certified or FDA approved. Please use your best judgement and discretion before wearing this mask and observe the social distancing and hygiene guidelines recommended by the CDC and public health departments. We do not claim that this mask is guaranteed to keep you protected from COVID-19.

Design

Easy to make, customized to fit and generates minimal waste.

Like folding a paper airplane

A sheet of Tyvek™ or any other textile is transformed from a 2D flat pattern into a 3D form with a few simple folds, like origami. No sewing required.


The entire mask weighs 0.48 oz (13.5g).

Custom fitting with an airtight seal

Since the respirator is assembled on site, the wearer can adjust it for a tight fit with no gaps. Tyvek™ is flexible enough to move with the wearer's skin so that facial expressions and talking won't introduce gaping or cause your glasses to fog.

3D printed filter assembly

The filter assembly holds an N95 or similar filter and twists and locks in place. The filter and mask are thrown away and in the plastic parts can be cleaned between each use (in lower-risk situations).

Materials

An adaptable design that uses materials commonly found in hospitals.

The materials below are recommended for those that work in healthcare. Please read the Safety section and consult with administrators before determining their use.

  • Tyvek™ (antimicrobial 1073B preferred)
  • Adhesive-backed neoprene (antimicrobial preferred)
  • N95 filter, Halyard H600, or fiberglass-free MERV 13+ air filter
  • PETG filament preferred, PLA or ABS acceptable
  • Any double-sided adhesive
  • Aluminum nosepiece
  • Straps of any kind - self adhesive bandage wrap works well
Assembly

Parts print in less than an hour, ships flat and assembles in minutes.

Fold into shape using the adhesive tabs, add a neoprene strip and adjust for an airtight seal. Insert the filter assembly with an N95 filter and tie on the straps. After use, throw away the filter and mask and sanitize the filter assembly.
